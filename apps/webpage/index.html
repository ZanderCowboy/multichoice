Multichoice

Organize your reading lists, to-dos, and collections in one beautifully simple app. Create tabs, add entries, and take control of what matters to you – all offline.

✨ Why Multichoice?

  • 📋Create and manage multiple tabs for any category
  • 🌗Gorgeous dark & light themes
  • 🔍Instant search across all your entries
  • 📤Export & import for easy backups
  • 📴Fully offline – your data stays on your device
